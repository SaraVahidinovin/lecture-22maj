Oprah Winfrey's Biography

About Oprah Winfrey

Oprah Gail Winfrey is an American talk show host, television producer, actress, author, and philanthropist. She is best known for her talk show, The Oprah Winfrey Show, which was the highest-rated television program of its kind in history and ran in national syndication for 25 years, from 1986 to 2011. Dubbed the "Queen of All Media", she was the richest African-American of the 20th century and was once the world's only black billionaire.

Top 3 Coolest Things Oprah Has Done

  • Launched her own television network, the Oprah Winfrey Network (OWN).
  • Created the Oprah's Book Club, which has made numerous books bestsellers.
  • Established the Oprah Winfrey Leadership Academy for Girls in South Africa.
